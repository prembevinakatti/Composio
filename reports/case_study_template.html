🤖 Agent Architecture

The research pipeline consists of autonomous agents powered by Composio SDK for tool orchestration.

  1. Research Agent: Utilizes Groq/Gemini with Composio web search tools to gather official documentation.
  2. Extraction: Instructor parses findings into a strict Pydantic `AppResearch` schema.
  3. Verification Agent: A secondary LLM pass reviews findings, flags inconsistencies, and assigns a confidence score.
